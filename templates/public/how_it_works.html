The season, end to end

Feel the flow. Three moves, one season.

01

Set up

Set up your group. Run the Charity Vote.

Name your comp, invite your team, set the entry fee. No payment until you're ready to launch. While the group fills, run the Charity Vote — everyone nominates, everyone votes, majority wins. That's where the season's money is headed before a single game is tipped.

02

Tip

Tip and track, every round.

AFL, AFLW, NRL and NRLW. Weekly tips, a live ladder, and the running charity total in the same place. State of Origin picks are worth four points; finals worth two. The Wall keeps the banter and the tally front and centre all season long.

03

Impact

Season done well.

Last grand final done. The Impact Report goes out: total raised, charity named, tipping results, the lot. One number your board can cite and your group can be proud of. Season done well.

Scoring

Points that mean something.

Simple enough for the casual tipper, with just enough weighting to keep the big rounds interesting.

1

One point

Correct tip

One point for every game you pick right, across all four codes. The bread and butter of the ladder.

2

Double

Finals

Finals tips are worth two points each. The run home is where ladders are won and lost.

4

Quadruple

State of Origin

Origin picks are worth four points. Three nights that can flip the whole comp on its head.

Roles

Two roles. One season.

Team Manager

Sets it up. Keeps it running.

Creates the comp, sets the entry fee, the base pledge and the matching cap. Runs the Charity Vote and invites the group. The one whose name is on it — in the good way.

Captain

Nominated. Then owns the season.

Voted in by the group to keep the energy up: chasing stragglers, stoking the Wall, and rallying the top-ups when the total needs a nudge. Every great comp has one.
