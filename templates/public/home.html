How the donation works

Three ways the money grows. Watch it flow.

It builds left to right across the season — tap any step to hold it and read.

1

Base pledge

Your organisation sets a donation amount at the start of the season.

Paid however suits you — upfront, monthly, or at season close. This is the floor: the number the group can already see climbing on day one.

2

Participant top-ups

Anyone in the group can add a voluntary top-up on top of their entry.

Entirely optional. Nobody's chased for it. But when the running total is visible all season on the ladder and the Wall, people give — and the average creeps up.

3

Organisation match

Your organisation matches top-ups dollar for dollar, up to a cap.

The group watches it happen in real time. That's the moment a tipping comp becomes a movement — and the total on the ladder is exactly what the charity receives.

Three separate flows. The subscription runs the platform — every donated dollar reaches the charity. Nothing skimmed.
